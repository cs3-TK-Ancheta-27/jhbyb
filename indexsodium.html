The prince and his most devoted follower

By:Tanya Khrystelle E. Ancheta

The prince, fallen from grace

The Ghost king, with a hidden face

In crimson robes and feathers so bright

They dance through the day and conguer the night

Through laughter and tears, their story unfolds

In the pages of history, their legend is told

In eachother's embrace, they found their charms

Last time it took them 800 years to fall inlove

Now it took them just a year to fall in each other's arms
